Throw-Up

Usually containing multiple colors, this style of grafitti is a more complex

Wildstyle

A wildstyle tag is an elaborate tag, done with multiple colors and characters.
Mostly consisting of arrows, curves, spikes, and other entities.

Heaven

A heaven tag touches on an important part of a grafitti tag, the spectacle effect. Having other become curious on how that tag got there.
